FAQ

Common queries & solutions

What platform do I trade on?▾
You trade on MetaTrader 5 (MT5) using a demo server account. MT5 is free to download on Windows, Mac, iOS and Android. Your login credentials are emailed automatically after payment — you can start trading within minutes.
What happens if I fail the challenge?▾
If you breach a drawdown limit your challenge is closed. You can purchase a new challenge at any time — many traders succeed on a second or third attempt. Each gives you a fresh account and a clean start.
Is my challenge fee refunded?▾
Yes — your full challenge fee is refunded as part of your first funded payout. If you paid €89 and your first payout is €800, you receive €889 total.
How and when do I get paid?▾
Payouts are processed monthly. Request a payout from your dashboard and receive 80% of your net profit via Wise or bank transfer within 3-5 business days. A one-time identity check is required before your first payout.
Can I trade news events or hold over the weekend?▾
Yes to both. We do not restrict news trading or weekend holding. Your strategy is yours — we only enforce the profit target and drawdown rules shown on this page.
Who is eligible to join?▾
PropCapital accepts traders from most countries worldwide. Some restrictions apply based on local regulations. If you are unsure, contact us via live chat before purchasing.
